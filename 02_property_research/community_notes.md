## Westbrook

### East Corridor
**Zip codes:** [add local zip]
Honestly, not somewhere we'd recommend right now. High crime relative to the rest of Westbrook,
lots of transient rental population, and not an area where you'd feel comfortable walking alone
at night. There's some redevelopment happening near the edge of the corridor but the core
stretch is still rough. We steer clients away from this one unless they specifically know the area
and are comfortable with it.

### Elmwood
**Zip codes:** [add local zip]
Great neighborhood — safe, walkable, strong community feel. Lots of parks, community gardens,
close to the university and downtown. Families love it. Homes don't sit long here so buyers need to be
pre-approved and ready to move. Older housing stock means you'll almost always get inspection
findings, so build that into your offer strategy.
